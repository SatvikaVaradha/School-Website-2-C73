Campus Life

School Entrance

Was established in 1937

This school is for girls aged from 11-18 and the only way to gain access is through the 11 plus. The school is constructed on a 17-acre (6.9 ha) site and has sporting facilities. A new building was completed in 2006 which provides a theatre, recording studio, a large Sports Hall, five additional science laboratories, and a further ICT suite.
